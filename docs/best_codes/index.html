About the construction (Quantum Tanner codes)

The codes tracked here are quantum Tanner codes (CSS LDPC codes) introduced by Leverrier–Zémor (FOCS 2022). Informally: you start from a small “base” CSS template built from a few short classical codes (“local codes”), then you lift it using a finite group G and two ordered multisets A, B in G. The lift acts by left and right regular actions of G, producing the large check matrices H_X and H_Z.

This dashboard currently focuses on the weight‑9 search regime where the local codes are the shortened Hamming [6,3,3] code (and its dual), up to column permutations.
